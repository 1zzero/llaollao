Company

We are the number one European frozen yogurt brand in the world.

The project was created in 2009 by Pedro Espinosa (current CEO), with the initial idea of rolling out a small network of stores on the Mediterranean coast, revolutionized the sector.

Such was the impact of concept on the market that in just two years llaollaoTM had opened more than 50 points of sale, celebrating the opening of the 100th 2 years later.

The following years were key for the consolidation of the brand at national and international level.

Committing to a premium quality, nutritionally balanced and completely customizable product thanks to the infinite toppings, in these 14 years of llaollao our customers, passionate about and loyal to the brand, have enjoyed our products in more than 390 locations around the world. Let's not forget that over this time that they, our customers, are the ones who give meaning and continuity to the brand.
